Online Ticket Booking (Official)

The Pakistan Railway ticketing system is fully computerised and online. Users can obtain computerised open tickets and advanced reservations directly from railway stations. Advanced reservations can also be made using the RABTA mobile app or the Pakistan Railways official website. Moreover, at major stations, TVMs (Ticket Vending Machines) are available for convenient ticket reservation.
