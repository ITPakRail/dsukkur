About Us

pakrail.online is a public information portal created to help passengers easily access Pakistan Railways information.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide clear, fast, and reliable railway information for travelers across Pakistan.

What We Provide

  • Train timing information
  • Seat availability and coach details
  • Live train tracking
  • Helpful travel guides

Important Notice

We do not sell tickets or process payments. All bookings must be made through official Pakistan Railways channels.

Why We Exist

Many users find official platforms confusing. pakrail.online simplifies railway information for the general public.
