Message from the Divisional Superintendent

Pakistan Railways, Sukkur Division is committed to continuously improving passenger services and station facilities to ensure a safe, comfortable, and reliable travel experience. We are actively working on the upgradation of infrastructure, cleanliness, information services, and passenger support systems across all stations of the division.

This digital portal has been developed to provide easy access to station information, facilities, and important updates for the public. It also serves as a platform for passengers to share their concerns, suggestions, or complaints.

If you experience any inconvenience during your journey or at any station, we encourage you to register your complaint through this portal. Your feedback is important to us and will be reviewed and addressed promptly by the concerned authorities.

Together, we aim to build a more efficient, transparent, and passenger-friendly railway system.
